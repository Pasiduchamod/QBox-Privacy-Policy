# Privacy Policy for QBox

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to QBox ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use the QBox mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information (Google Sign-In Users)
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Google account ID

This information is used solely for authentication and account management purposes.

### 2. One-Time Room Users
For users who create one-time rooms without signing in:
- Lecturer name (provided by you)
- No personal identification information is collected or stored

### 3. Room and Question Data
- Room names and codes
- Questions submitted (anonymous for students)
- Anonymous student tags (generated randomly, stored locally)
- Question upvotes and reports
- Room activity timestamps

### 4. Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage statistics (crashes, performance)
- Network connectivity status

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate users and manage accounts
- Create and manage Q&A rooms
- Enable anonymous question submission
- Display questions and manage room activities
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Ensure security and prevent abuse

## Data Storage and Security

- User data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas servers
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Google Sign-In follows OAuth 2.0 security standards
- Student questions remain anonymous with randomly generated tags
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Disclose your information except as described below

We **MAY** share information:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View your profile information
- **Edit:** Update your name and profile details
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Anonymity:** Use one-time rooms without creating an account
- **Regenerate Tag:** Change your anonymous student tag at any time

### Data Deletion
To delete your account and data:
1. Contact us at axlesolutionsinfo@gmail.com
2. We will delete your data within 30 days of verification

## One-Time Rooms

One-time rooms automatically expire after 1 hour. Associated data (questions, room information) may be retained for a limited period for system integrity but will be permanently deleted within 90 days.

## Children's Privacy

QBox is designed for educational purposes and may be used by students of various ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Sign-In
We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to data collected during the sign-in process. Review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### MongoDB Atlas
We use MongoDB Atlas for data storage. MongoDB's security and privacy practices apply to data stored on their servers.

## Anonymous Questions

Student questions are submitted anonymously using randomly generated tags (e.g., "Panda#1234"). These tags are stored locally on your device and can be regenerated at any time. We cannot link anonymous questions to specific users.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **One-Time Rooms:** Deleted after 90 days
- **Questions:** Retained as long as the room exists or 90 days after room closure

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through:
- App notifications
- Email (for registered users)
- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of QBox after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
